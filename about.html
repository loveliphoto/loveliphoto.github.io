About LOVE LI Photography

Betty bought her first "fancy" camera back in 2012 and hasn't put down the camera since. After the Li(ang) Wedding in October 2014, Ben began to join Betty on photoshoots as a proud second shooter. Ben and Betty have been dreaming about this future ever since. As we reflect on our marriage and the future of this passion project, the word we keep coming back to is "Love."
